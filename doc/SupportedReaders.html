Readers supported by OpenCT

This list contains all drivers and which readers are supported by that driver. Please also check the RecentlyTested? page, as some readers might be broken.

  • cardman: Omnikey Cardman
  • ccid: all usb readers conforming to the ccid standard.
  • e-gate: Schlumberger/Axalto e-gate tokens
  • etoken: Aladdin eToken PRO
  • eutron: Eutron "CryptoIdendity" IT-SEC
  • gempc: Gemplus Readers
  • ikey2032: Rainbow iKey 2032
  • ikey3000: Rainbow iKey 3000
  • kaan: Kobil Kaan Professional and Telesec B1
  • smartboard: Cherry Smartboard
  • towitoko: Towitoko Chipdrive mini, Kartenzwerg, Kartenzwerg II, Chipdrive external, internal, twin

If your smart card reader isn't listed above, here is a list of websites with drivers for PC/SC-Lite. Maybe one of them has support for your reader:

  • http://www.linuxnet.com/sourcedrivers.html Collection of source drivers for PC/SC-Lite
  • http://pcsclite.alioth.debian.org/ccid.html CCID driver for PC/SC-Lite
