OpenCT Contact Information

Please don't contact any of the OpenCT developers directly, unless you have a good reason. Use one of the mailing lists instead, this is much easier for both of us. For an in depth explanation read [http://www.eyrie.org/~eagle/faqs/questions.html this page].

Mailing lists

  • opensc-annouce - Announcements of new releases, bugfixes and security warnings
  • opensc-devel - Discussion of developement issues for OpenSC, OpenCT and SCB
  • opensc-user - Discussion of end-user questions for OpenSC, OpenCT and SCB
  • opensc-commits - commit notifications for all projects hosted at opensc.org

To subscribe, unsubscribe or browse the archive, please visit our mailing list manager.

You need to subscribe to these mailing lists, if you want to post to them. But you can subscribe and then turn receiving mails off, which might be nice in case you use gmane or a similar service to read the mailing lists. Outside postings are not silently dropped, but also not forwarded to the mailing list. Instead a moderator needs to review every posting and decide, this might add a delay of several hours or even days.

Note you can also send bug reports to bugs@opensc.org in case you don't want to subscribe to the mailing lists. It might be also good to send security sensitive information to this address.
